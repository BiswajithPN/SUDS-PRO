INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADDING PRODUCT IMAGES
========================================

1. Place your product images in this 'images' folder
2. Remove the background from your images and ensure they have a white background
3. Recommended image names:
   - liquid-detergent-white.jpg (or .png)
   - liquid-detergent-blue.jpg (or .png)
   - stain-remover.jpg (or .png)
   - floor-cleaner.jpg (or .png)
   - phenyl.jpg (or .png)
   - fabric-conditioner.jpg (or .png)
   - dish-wash.jpg (or .png)

4. Image requirements:
   - Clean white background
   - High quality and clear
   - Recommended size: 300x500px or similar aspect ratio
   - Format: JPG or PNG

5. After adding images, refresh the website to see them displayed

Note: If an image is missing, a placeholder will be shown. You can also update image paths in the Manage panel.
