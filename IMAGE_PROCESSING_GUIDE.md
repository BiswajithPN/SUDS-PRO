# Image Processing Guide for SUDS PRO Products

## Background Removal & Enhancement

To make your product images look professional with transparent backgrounds, follow these steps:

### Option 1: Online Tools (Recommended for Quick Processing)
1. **Remove.bg** (https://www.remove.bg/)
   - Upload your HEIC/PNG images
   - Automatically removes background
   - Download as PNG with transparent background

2. **Canva** (https://www.canva.com/)
   - Upload image → Effects → Background Remover
   - Enhance quality and adjust brightness/contrast

### Option 2: AI-Powered Tools
1. **Adobe Express** (https://www.adobe.com/express/)
   - Free background remover
   - AI-powered enhancement

2. **Photopea** (https://www.photopea.com/) - Free Photoshop alternative
   - Open image
   - Use Magic Wand or Select Subject
   - Delete background
   - Export as PNG

### Images That Need Processing:
1. `fabric conditioner.heic` → Convert to `fabric conditioner.png` (remove background)
2. `phenyl.heic` → Convert to `phenyl.png` (remove background)

### Enhancement Tips:
- Remove speckled floor and wall backgrounds
- Ensure product is centered
- Use clean white or transparent background
- Adjust brightness/contrast for professional look
- Save as high-quality PNG (transparent background)

### After Processing:
- Replace the HEIC files with PNG versions
- Ensure filenames match exactly:
  - `fabric conditioner.png`
  - `phenyl.png`
- Images will automatically display on the website
